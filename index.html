About

The Short Version

I'm a Karen McKellin International Leader of Tomorrow Scholar at UBC, pursuing a combined major in Physics and Computer Science. I build simulations that push the boundaries of what's possible in space tech and autonomous systems.

Currently Building

  • • Active radiation shielding for spacecraft @ PARSEC
  • • Autonomous rover arm with RL @ UBC Rover
  • • CubeSat thermal simulations @ UBC Orbit

My Approach

I believe in simulation-first design—building digital twins before physical prototypes. This methodology has helped me optimize everything from magnetic shielding configurations to electromagnetic launchers.
